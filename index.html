Financial Math

Net Present Value (NPV):

\[ \mathrm{NPV} = \sum_{t=0}^{T} \frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^{t}} \]

Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC):

\[ \mathrm{WACC} = \frac{E}{D+E} r_e + \frac{D}{D+E} r_d (1-\tau) \]
